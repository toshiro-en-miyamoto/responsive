The justify-content property is also the property used to space items out along the main axis.

  • space-between creates an equal amount of space between our items
  • space-around creates an equal amount of space around the items
  • space-evenly distributes the items evenly on the main axis

By adding the align-content property with a value of space-between, the items take as much space as they need for their content; then, the remaining space is distributed between the items. This works in the same way as justify-content on the main axis.

.cards {
    margin: 0 -10px;
    padding: 0;
    list-style: none;
    display: flex;
    flex-wrap: wrap;
    height: 50vh;
    align-content: stretch;
